barak.voigt.voigt_slow¶

barak.voigt.voigt_slow(a, u)[source]¶

Calculate the voigt function to very high accuracy.

Uses numerical integration, so is slow. Answer is correct to 20 significant figures.

Note this needs mpmath or sympy to be installed.
