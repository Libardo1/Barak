LMC Extinction law from Gordon et al. 2003 LMC supershell sample.

Parameters :

wa : array_like

One or more wavelengths in Angstroms.
Returns :

Ext : ExtinctionCurve instance

A(lambda)/A(V) at each wavelength and Rv for the LMC supershell sample.

Notes

At far IR wavelengths, a MW extinction curve is assumed.
