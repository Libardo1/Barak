barak.coord.check_ra_dec¶

barak.coord.check_ra_dec(ra, dec)[source]¶

Check 0 <= RA < 360 and -90 <= Dec <= 90.

Raises a ValueError outside these limits.

Parameters :

ra, dec : floats or arrays of floats

RA and Dec in degrees.
