barak.extinction.MW_Cardelli89¶

barak.extinction.MW_Cardelli89(wa, Rv=3.1)[source]¶

Milky Way Extinction law from Cardelli et al. 1989.

Parameters :

wa : array_like

One or more wavelengths in Angstroms

Rv : float (default 3.1)

R(V). The default is for the diffuse ISM, Rv of 5 is generally used for dense molecular clouds.
Returns :

AlamAv, Rv : ndarray, float

A(lambda) / A(V) at each wavelength and Rv.

Notes

A power law extrapolation is used if there are any wavelengths past the IR or far UV limits of the Cardelli Law.

References

http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1989ApJ...345..245C
