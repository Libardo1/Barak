barak.coord.s2dec¶

barak.coord.s2dec(ra, dec)[source]¶

Convert a sexigesimal ra and dec (or list of ras and decs) to decimal degrees.

Parameters :

ra, dec: str or arrays of str, shape (N,) :

The RA and Dec in ‘hour:min:s’ ‘deg:min:s’ format. The separators may be spaces or colons.
Returns :

ra, dec: floats or arrays of floats, shape (N,) :

The RA and Dec in degrees.
