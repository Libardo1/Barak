barak.spec.air2vac_Morton¶

barak.spec.air2vac_Morton(airw)[source]¶

Convert air wavelengths in Angstroms to vacuum wavelengths.

Uses linear interpolation of the inverse transformation for vac2air_Morton. The fractional error (wa - watrue) / watrue introduced by interpolation is < 1e-9.

Only valid for wa > 2000 Angstroms.
