barak.sed.calc_extinction_Calzetti¶

barak.sed.calc_extinction_Calzetti(wa, EBmV)[source]¶

Find the extinction as a function of wavelength for the given E(B-V) using the relation from Calzetti et al. 2000 (ApJ, 533, 682). R_v’ = 4.05 is assumed (see equation (5) of Calzetti et al.) E(B-V) is the extinction in the stellar continuum.

The wavelength array wa must be in Angstroms and sorted lowest -> highest.

Returns reddening such that:

extincted_flux = unextincted_flux * reddening
