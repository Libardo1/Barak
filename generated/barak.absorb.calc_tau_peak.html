barak.absorb.calc_tau_peak¶

barak.absorb.calc_tau_peak(logN, b, trans)[source]¶

Estimate of the peak optical depth of a transition assuming we are on the linear part of the curve of growth.

logN is log10 of column density in cm^-2 b is b parameter in km/s trans is a transisition, e.e.g ‘HI 1215’
